## Context

What is the problem or force driving this decision? What constraints apply
(security, compliance, performance, team, deadline)? State facts, not solutions.

## Decision

What we decided to do, stated in active voice. Be specific enough that someone can
act on it.

## Consequences

- Positive: what improves.
- Negative / trade-offs: what we accept or give up.
- Security/compliance implications, if any.

## Rollback

## Risk

any exception records if a risk is accepted.
